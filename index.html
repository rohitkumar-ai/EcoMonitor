Air Quality Monitoring

Our advanced air quality monitoring systems provide real-time data on various pollutants including PM2.5, PM10, ozone, nitrogen dioxide, sulfur dioxide, and carbon monoxide. Using a network of sensors and satellite data, we offer:

  • Hourly updates on air quality indices
  • Pollution source identification
  • Health risk assessments
  • Historical trend analysis
  • Custom alerts for sensitive groups

This technology helps governments, businesses, and individuals make informed decisions to reduce exposure and improve air quality.
